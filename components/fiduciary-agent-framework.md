# Fiduciary Agent Framework (FAF)

AI agents are not legal entities. They cannot sign term sheets, act as fiduciaries, or be held liable in a court of law. Handing an AI the private keys to a treasury without a legal wrapper is an unquantifiable liability for human operators.

The **Fiduciary Agent Framework (FAF)** is the legal structuring counterpart to OVE.

## The Legal-Technical Bridge

FAF does not attempt to grant an AI legal personhood. Instead, it creates an impenetrable legal-technical wrapper around the code:

1. **The Agent as Property:** Utilizing ERC-6551 (Token Bound Accounts), the AI agent exists on-chain as a tokenized asset (an NFT).
2. **The Legal Anchor:** That NFT is legally owned by a recognized, real-world entity, such as an LLC or a human General Partner.
3. **Bounded Liability:** Because the Open Venture Engine (OVE) mathematically guarantees the agent cannot break its hard-coded policy, the human partners can safely wrap that code in a legal entity without assuming infinite risk.
